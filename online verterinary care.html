Welcome to our Online Veterinary Care platform!

Functionalities:













Register/Login

Users can create an account or log in with their credentials to access all the available functionalities.

Schedule Appointments

Users can schedule appointments with veterinarians, selecting the desired date and time.

Access Medical Records

Users can view and manage their pet's medical history and records.

Telemedicine Consultations

Users can request telemedicine consultations with veterinarians, allowing them to discuss their pet's health remotely.

Order Medications and Supplies

Users can order medications, pet food, and other supplies conveniently through the platform.

Find Veterinarians and Clinics

Users can search for veterinarians and clinics near their location, access reviews and ratings, and make appointments.

Get Pet Health Tips and Articles

Users can access a range of pet health tips, articles, and educational resources to help them take better care of their pets.
